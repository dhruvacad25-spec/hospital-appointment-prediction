The Cost of an Empty Slot

A missed appointment is never just one missed appointment.

It's a doctor's hour with no patient, a delayed diagnosis for someone on the waiting list, and a line item that hospitals absorb quietly, again and again.

Lost clinical time

Every unattended slot is a reserved hour of specialist time that can't be recovered or reassigned on short notice.

Longer waiting lists

Patients who need that same slot stay queued longer, while the original booking goes to no one.

Compounding cost

Across a 10,000-visit calendar, even a modest no-show rate adds up to real revenue and resourcing loss.

How The Model Reads a Booking

From intake form to risk score, in four steps.

The same pipeline that trained on 10,000 historical records runs underneath every prediction you request.

01

Patient & visit details are captured

Age, BMI, department, booking lead time, and appointment history are collected from the intake form.

02

Categorical fields are encoded

Gender, department, and appointment type are label-encoded to match the format the model was trained on.

03

The KNN classifier scores the visit

The trained model compares the new booking against its nearest neighbors in the historical dataset.

04

A risk read is returned instantly

You see a clear attend / at-risk / no-show signal, with the leading contributing factors.
